ICYMI: PUP-STC BSIT Graduating Students Hold Graduation Pictorial
The Cloud Sentinel
Published Jul 23, 2025 2:00 PM PHT
Graduating BSIT Students in preparation for their graduation pictorial. Contributed Photo.

STO TOMAS CITY, BATANGAS — In a milestone moment for the graduating class of 2025, the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) students of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines Sto. Tomas Branch (PUP-STC) gathered for their official graduation pictorial held last July 12, 2025, in partnership with Relans Digital Photography.

The event was organized by the PUP-STC Graduating Class Steering Committee, led by Mc Genrev Egar from the BSIT program. Coordination of the graduation photo sessions was spearheaded by Phoebe Caye Nocum, Committee Head for Graduation Photos, from the Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship (BSENT) program.

Graduating students posed in traditional toga, Barong/Filipiniana, creative attire, and group shots, capturing a mix of formal and personalized memories as they near the finish line of their college journey.

While the official graduation day and venue are yet to be confirmed, students and their families are encouraged to stay updated through the PUP STC Graduating Class Updates Facebook page.

According to university figures, a total of 594 students across 12 academic programs are expected to graduate, pending final confirmation. Of these, 57 are from the BSIT program, highlighting the growing contribution of tech graduates from the campus.

As graduation draws near, the pictorial serves as a celebratory prelude to the students' years of hard work, resilience, and achievement — a moment now beautifully preserved in photos.
