COMELEC releases two partylists for the upcoming PUP Sto. Tomas CSC Elections; 3 candidates from ComSoc
The Cloud Sentinel
Published Oct 13, 2025 11:46:00 AM PHT
PUP Halalan 2025 banner. PUP STC COMELEC Facebook page.

The PUP Sto. Tomas Commission on Elections (COMELEC) has officially released the two partylists who will run in the upcoming Central Student Council (CSC) Elections, the highest form of student representation on campus.

The two partylists are Hirang and Kalasag, both fielding candidates for various CSC positions.

For the presidential race, Jasmine Narvaez of the Society of Electrical Engineering Students (SEES) will represent Hirang, while Edwil Grae Zapata of the Institute of Technology (ITECH) will represent Kalasag.

Among the official candidates are three members of the Computer Society (ComSoc) who will be running for key CSC positions:

Hirang Partylist

  • Liam Mathew Palomo (BSIT 2-1) - CSC Councilor
    Agenda: Enhancing Resume Credentials Through Certification Opportunities — aims to improve students' employability by providing certification programs aligned with their academic tracks and career goals.

  • Ashley Bianca Cruz (BSIT 3-1) - CSC College Representative (ComSoc)
    Agendas: Digital Edge Summit: Skills for the Modern Student — a one-day training to equip students with essential digital and productivity skills; and Connect: The Inter-Org Solutions Jam — a collaborative sprint to develop digital solutions for campus organizations.

Kalasag Partylist

  • Rhianne Gabriel Magpantay (BSIT 3-1) - CSC Councilor
    Agendas: Festive Spirit: Paskuhan sa PUP — a campus-wide celebration promoting unity and school spirit; CONNECT (Communication, Openness, Networking, Engagement, Collaboration, Transparency) — an initiative to strengthen feedback channels between the student body and the council; KAPWA: Paskong handog ng kabataan - A christmas outreach and gift-giving program; and MIND PAUSE: Mental Health Initiative - aims to give every student a break amidst academic stress.

According to the PUP Sto. Tomas Commission on Elections (COMELEC), the official Election Season calendar is as follows:

  • October 13 - Start of Campaign Period
  • October 17 - End of Campaign Period / Miting de Avance (venue TBA)
  • October 18 - Election Day
  • October 19 - Proclamation of Winners
  • November 4 - Oath-taking of elected officers

The 2025 CSC Elections mark another milestone in empowering Iskolars ng Bayan to choose leaders who will represent their voices and uphold student welfare and progress.
