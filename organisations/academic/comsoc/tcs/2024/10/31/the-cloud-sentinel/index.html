Strength in Innovation: Computer Society's The Cloud Sentinel Unveils Official Logo
Denmar Redondo | The Cloud Sentinel
Published Oct 31, 2024 3:32 PM PHT
The official logo of the Cloud Sentinel. Christian James Torillo.

The Cloud Sentinel, The official publication of the Computer Society under its publication committee, officially launches its new logo.

Designed by Christian James Torrillo, head of ComSoc's design committee, the publication is thrilled to present a new logo element: the letter "C," which stands for "Cloud." The emblem, displayed in a striking mustard yellow and resembles a sturdy streetlight, represents the publication's dedication to providing reliable and accurate information.

TCS seeks to highlight the complexities of our society, much like a streetlight aids people to find their way. The vivid mustard yellow color conveys hope and clarity, highlighting the publication's commitment to providing knowledge that empowers.

The logo also includes the eye-catching letter "S," which stands for "Sentinel." This element is meant to resemble a beacon of light, with its stark white color standing out to reflect clarity, insight, and the guiding role the publication aims to play in the constantly changing information landscape. The logo also symbolizes a spearhead, ready to strike for truth without fear and hesitation, assuring its loyalty to the Iskolar ng Bayan.

The Cloud Sentinel is dedicated as a reliable source of information as it expands, making sure that readers have the direction they require to traverse the clouds of information. The Computer Society is the official academic organization of BSIT Students of PUP Sto Tomas.
