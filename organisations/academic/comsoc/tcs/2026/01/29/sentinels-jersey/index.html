Black and Gold, Reimagined: The Sentinels Release COMSOC's Latest Competitive Jersey
The Cloud Sentinel
Published Jan 29, 2026 04:16:00 PM PHT

The Computer Society's competitive team brand, The Sentinels, steps into the 2026-2027 esports season with the release of its latest official jersey — a refined take on the team's established black and gold identity that blends performance design with symbolic detail.

Remaining faithful to COMSOC's signature colors, the new jersey introduces sharper lines and dynamic patterns that convey movement and intensity. Flowing angular elements in vibrant yellow cut across a textured black base, creating a visual rhythm that mirrors the fast-paced nature of competitive gaming while maintaining a clean, professional aesthetic.

Prominently displayed on the front are the SENTINELS name and COMSOC, affirming the team's role as the Computer Society's official esports representatives. The shoulders bear “COMPUTER" and “SOCIETY," reinforcing the academic organization behind the competitive squad. Subtle identifiers such as the squad label “VOX" and the game marker “VALO" reflect the structure of the team across various titles, while the back of the jersey is reserved for each player's in-game name, giving every uniform a personalized touch.

Designed by Marcus Marasigan, Head of the Design Committee, the jersey builds upon The Sentinels' existing visual language while introducing modern elements that elevate both form and function. Rather than redefining the brand, the concept refines it — presenting a look that feels familiar yet forward-looking.

For the upcoming season, The Sentinels will continue to compete in Tekken, Valorant, Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB), and Call of Duty: Mobile (CODM), carrying COMSOC's colors across multiple competitive arenas.
